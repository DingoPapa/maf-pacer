How to use

A run

  1. Open in Bluefy (for live HR), wear the Polar H10, quit Polar Beat, tap Connect Polar H10.
  2. Pick a workout → Start. Match the treadmill to the big Speed and Incline numbers; keep HR under the ceiling.
  3. Deviate when you need to. If HR drifts over 140, slow the treadmill and tap the – Speed button to match. Any change (speed or incline) turns amber and is logged with the exact time and your BPM.
  4. On hold/test stages, use the steppers to dial in and record the speed you actually held.
  5. Finish → Avg/Max HR auto-fill → Copy session log and paste it to me. It includes per-stage HR, every adjustment, and BPM stats.

Important

  • Auto-Lock = Never (iPhone Settings → Display) and keep this page in front — backgrounding stops the timers and can drop the HR stream.
  • The mph/incline numbers are starting points. HR is the boss: stay under 140 and let pace fall if it climbs.
  • The HR number turns amber + soft double-beep as you near the cap, then red + alarm if you go over — ease off when you hear it.
  • No Bluefy? Cues, timers, adjustments and logging still work in Safari — just type your Avg/Max HR from Polar Beat at the end.
  • Music: add your Suno MP3s in the Music section (Home) — stored on your device, never uploaded. They play during runs and duck automatically when the over-cap alarm sounds, so you always hear it.
