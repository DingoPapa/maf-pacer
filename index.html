How to use

All three on one screen

  1. Open this page in the Bluefy browser (free on the App Store) from its https link — not Safari, and not a downloaded file. That's what lets it read your strap and play video.
  2. Wear the Polar H10. On Home, tap Connect Polar H10 and pick it. (Don't run Polar Beat at the same time — let one app own the strap.)
  3. Paste a YouTube link (non-music, embeddable) and tap Set video.
  4. Pick a workout → Start. You'll see the video, your live BPM, and the speed/incline/timer cues together. Tap the video once to start its sound.
  5. Keep HR under the ceiling; if it goes over, the number turns red + beeps — drop the treadmill speed.
  6. At the end, your Avg/Max HR are filled in automatically. Tap Copy session log and paste it to me with your Polar Beat screenshots.

Important

  • Set Auto-Lock to Never (Settings → Display) and keep this page in front the whole run — backgrounding it stops the timers and can drop the HR stream.
  • Keep the video inline (don't tap fullscreen) so the cues stay visible.
  • Ads play on monetized videos; the cues keep running through them.
  • Smoke-test a new video for ~5 min in Bluefy before trusting it on a long run.

No Bluefy? Still works.

In plain Safari the workout cues, timers and logging all work — you just won't get live HR or the embedded video. Glance at Polar Beat for HR and type your Avg/Max at the end.
